## assumptions

- The user expects the infographic to be a static image, not interactive.
- The source for current market prices will be the manufacturer's official online store for the United States region.
- The creation date will be used as the reference point for 'current' pricing.
- Neutral phone color defaults to Silver, as a widely acceptable neutral option.
- The infographic is for digital use and does not require print-ready formats.
- All spec data will be gathered from reliable public sources (e.g., official product pages).

## userStories

- As a tech enthusiast, I want to quickly compare key specifications and pricing of the two flagship phones at a glance.
- As a content creator, I need an ultra-high resolution, visually appealing infographic for use in video thumbnails and social media.
- As a viewer, I expect accurate and up-to-date technical specifications, including all RAM/storage variants and their prices.
- As a designer, I require a precise layout with equal column distribution, bold titles, and clear typography.

## acceptanceCriteria

- Infographic must have a clean white background with a 4:5 aspect ratio.
- Left column contains Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra; right column contains iPhone 17 Pro Max.
- Each phone is represented with a photorealistic render in a neutral color (default silver).
- Phone titles are in bold red text.
- Specifications listed: display, processor, OS, cameras, battery, charging, weight, security, price, RAM/storage.
- All RAM/storage variants for each phone are displayed, with corresponding current market price for each variant.
- Prices reflect current market value from an agreed source (e.g., manufacturer's official store).
- Layout uses clean, balanced spacing and modern tech-review typography.
- Subtle shadows and sharp text for YouTube thumbnail quality.
- No additional branding, watermarks, or call-to-action overlays.
- Final deliverable is a high-resolution static image (PNG).
